Chicken and Noodles

"Mostly" home-made bowl of chicken and noodles soup

This is a quick and easy lunch or dinner recipe for chicken and noodles soup. It should take you no more than 30 minutes to whip up from ingredients to ready for eating.

Ingredients

  • 3 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (26 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups diced, cooked chicken breast
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 (9 ounce) packages frozen egg noodles

Directions

  1. Combine chicken broth, and both condensed soups into a large pot.
  2. Add diced chicken breast.
  3. Season with onion powder, seasoning salt, and garlic powder.
  4. Bring to a boil on high heat and then add egg noodles.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer until desired noodle consistency.
